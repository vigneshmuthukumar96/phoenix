Application Guidelines

Calculate quantity if water required to fill pipelines (Starting from tank outlet to endpoints) using following Formulates or Examples below.
Total Water required in Litres =0.77 x (Radius of Pipe in inches) Z Total length of pipe in feet

Diameter of Pipe Water holding capacity of 1ft Pipe
0.5 inch 49ml
0.75inch 108ml
1 inch 193m
  • Empty water tank and pipeline completely and close all th ends.
  • Fill the calculated quantity of water plus 10% extra in the water tank.
  • Add secur-all @ 25ml/ltr of water ( if descaling in presence of birds) or 50ml/ltr of water (in empty Sheds).
  • Open water tank outlet and allow Secur-all treated water to flow through pipelines till its end point.
  • Allow contact time of 3 Hours and fill water tank with sanitized water.
  • Open end caps and ensure the pipeline is flushed out with normal sanitized water from water tank for 2-3minutes.
  • The pipeline is now descaled/Sanitized for use.
  • Continue with normal water treatment schedule.
  • Perform Descaling preferably during night hours.
